Trenton's Learning Method

  • Immersion: Watch anime, dramas, or YouTube videos entirely in Japanese with Japanese subtitles.
  • Comprehensible Input: Focus on understanding content naturally through listening and reading.
  • Shadowing: Repeat what native speakers say to improve pronunciation and rhythm.
  • Sentence Mining: Extract useful sentences from media and add them to Anki.
  • Spaced Repetition: Use Anki to retain vocab long-term based on real content you encounter.
